### How to Fill In

- **ID**: Ticket ID from your project tracker (e.g., PROJ-1234)
- **Title**: Short descriptive title
- **Points**: Story points or T-shirt size estimate
- **Owner**: Single person responsible (not "the team")
- **Dependencies**: Other story IDs, external teams, or "None"
- **Status**: Not Started / In Progress / Done / Blocked / Carried Over

### Validation Checks

- [ ] Every story has a single owner assigned
- [ ] Every story has an estimate
- [ ] Total commitment is at or below team capacity
- [ ] Maintenance items are at least 15% of total commitment
- [ ] All dependencies are identified with mitigation plans
- [ ] Carryover items are listed first and accounted for in capacity
