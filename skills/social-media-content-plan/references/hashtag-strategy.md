# Hashtag Strategy

## Reach Tier Taxonomy

Organize every hashtag into one of four tiers based on post volume (approximate monthly posts using that tag). Mix tiers deliberately — never stack all broad or all niche.

### Tier 1: Broad (1M+ posts)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Post volume | Over 1 million posts using this tag |
| Examples | #marketing, #startup, #leadership, #technology, #design |
| Reach potential | High impressions, but extreme competition — your post is buried within seconds |
| Rule | Use at most **1 per post**. Provides discoverability signal to the algorithm but won't drive meaningful engagement alone. |
| When to use | When you want category-level association. Pair with mid and niche tags for actual reach. |

### Tier 2: Mid (100K-1M posts)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Post volume | 100,000 to 1 million posts |
| Examples | #contentmarketing, #saas, #productdesign, #devtools, #remotework |
| Reach potential | Good balance of volume and visibility. Your post has a realistic chance of appearing in tag feeds. |
| Rule | Use **2-3 per post**. These are the workhorses of your hashtag strategy. |
| When to use | Match to your content pillar. These should align directly with your niche. |

### Tier 3: Niche (10K-100K posts)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Post volume | 10,000 to 100,000 posts |
| Examples | #b2bsaas, #devtoolsstartup, #plgstrategy, #uxwriting, #founderstory |
| Reach potential | Lower volume but higher engagement rate. Audience is more targeted and more likely to interact. |
| Rule | Use **2-3 per post**. These drive the most qualified engagement. |
| When to use | When your content speaks to a specific community. Niche tags signal expertise. |

### Tier 4: Branded (Custom)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Post volume | Created by you, starts at zero |
| Examples | #YourBrandName, #YourProductLaunch, #YourEventName |
| Reach potential | Zero organic discoverability initially. Builds over time as a content collection. |
| Rule | Use **1 per post** (optional). Consistent use creates a searchable archive of your content. |
| When to use | Product launches, campaigns, events, or ongoing series. Track usage in analytics. |

## Tier Mix Per Platform

| Platform | Recommended Mix | Total Tags |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | 1 Broad + 2 Mid + 1 Niche + 1 Branded | 3-5 |
| X / Twitter | 1 Mid + 1 Niche (or 1 Mid + 1 Branded) | 1-2 |
| Instagram | 1 Broad + 3-5 Mid + 3-5 Niche + 1 Branded | 5-15 (in first comment) |
| TikTok | 1 Broad + 2 Mid + 1-2 Niche | 3-5 |

## Building a Hashtag Bank

1. **Audit competitors** — Note which hashtags top-performing posts in your niche use. Categorize by tier.
2. **Check volume** — Use platform search or a tool (e.g., Hashtagify, RiteTag, or native search) to estimate post volume. Classify into tiers.
3. **Group by pillar** — Create a hashtag set for each content pillar. Each set should contain tags from all four tiers.
4. **Rotate within sets** — Do not use the exact same combination on every post. Rotate 2-3 tags per post to avoid appearing spammy to algorithms.

## Hashtag Bank Template

| Pillar | Broad (1) | Mid (2-3) | Niche (2-3) | Branded (1) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Thought leadership | #leadership | #founderlife, #startuplessons | #b2bfounder, #saasfounder | #[YourBrand] |
| Product updates | #technology | #saas, #productlaunch | #devtools, #buildinpublic | #[YourProduct] |
| Educational | #marketing | #contentmarketing, #growthhacking | #plgstrategy, #demandgen | #[YourSeries] |

## Performance Review Cadence

| Frequency | Action |
|---|---|
| Weekly | Note which posts outperformed. Record their hashtag combinations. |
| Monthly | Compare reach and engagement by hashtag set. Retire any tag that consistently underperforms. |
| Quarterly | Refresh the hashtag bank. Remove stale tags, add trending niche tags, verify volume tiers still accurate. |

## Anti-Patterns

- **Stuffing broad tags** — Five broad hashtags means zero niche signal. Your post competes with millions and reaches nobody specific.
- **Using banned or flagged tags** — Platforms shadow-ban certain hashtags periodically. Check before using.
- **Identical tags on every post** — Algorithms may flag repetitive hashtag use as spam. Rotate within your bank.
- **Hashtags in the middle of copy** — On LinkedIn and X, hashtags break reading flow. Place them at the end of the post or in a comment (Instagram).
