# Content Pillar-to-Platform Format Matrix

Map each content pillar to the best-performing format per platform. Not every pillar works on every platform — mark cells "N/A" when the fit is poor.

## Matrix

| Pillar | LinkedIn | X / Twitter | Instagram | TikTok |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Thought leadership | Long-form post (800-1500 chars). Hook in first 2 lines, personal insight, end with a question. | Thread (5-8 posts). Bold claim in Tweet 1, evidence in body, CTA in final tweet. | Carousel (8-10 slides). One idea per slide, large text, branded template. | Talking-head video (30-60s). Hot take + explanation. |
| Product updates | Feature post + screenshot/GIF. Describe the problem solved, not just the feature. | Single post + GIF/image. Lead with user benefit, not feature name. | Reel (15-30s) showing the feature in action, or Story with swipe-up link. | Short demo video (15-30s). Show the before/after. |
| Customer stories | Case study excerpt. Quote the customer directly, include a specific metric. | Quote-format post: "Customer said X" + your 1-sentence commentary. | Video testimonial (30-60s Reel) or quote graphic. | N/A (unless customer participates in video). |
| Behind the scenes | Team spotlight or "how we work" post. Humanize the company. | Casual observation or hot take about the process. Keep it conversational. | Photo of the team, office, or work-in-progress. Reel of a day-in-the-life. | Day-in-the-life or "POV: working at [Company]" format. |
| Educational / How-to | Step-by-step post or PDF carousel (document post). | Thread with numbered tips. Each tweet is one actionable step. | Carousel tutorial (1 step per slide, 8-12 slides). | Tutorial video (30-60s). Show the process on screen. |
| Industry news / Commentary | Opinion post reacting to news. State the news in 1 sentence, then your take. | Quote-post the news source with a one-line take. Or reply thread. | N/A (news commentary underperforms on visual-first platforms). | Reaction/stitch video responding to the news clip. |
| Community / Engagement | Poll or open question. "What's your take on X?" | Poll, question, or "hot take — agree or disagree?" | Story poll/quiz sticker. Or carousel with "save this" hook. | Duet/stitch or trending sound with a niche twist. |

## How to Use This Matrix

1. **Choose your pillars** — Most brands need 3-5 pillars. Pick the ones that align with your business goals and audience interests.
2. **Map to platforms** — For each pillar, use the format from this matrix that matches the platform. Do not cross-post the same format everywhere.
3. **Set ratios** — Decide what percentage of posts come from each pillar. A common split: 40% educational, 25% thought leadership, 20% product, 15% community.
4. **Build the calendar** — Assign pillar + platform + format to each slot in your weekly schedule.

## Notes

- Formats change as platforms evolve. Review this matrix quarterly and adjust based on your analytics.
- "N/A" means the pillar-platform combination typically underperforms — not that it is impossible. If your audience data says otherwise, override.
- Always adapt the tone to the platform even when the pillar is the same. A LinkedIn thought leadership post and a TikTok hot take cover the same topic but sound completely different.
