## Production Workflow

```
Stage 1: Idea       → Added to Notion backlog with pillar tag and target persona
Stage 2: Outline    → Approved by editor within 2 business days
Stage 3: Draft      → Author delivers full draft within 5 business days
Stage 4: Review     → Technical review by engineer + copy edit (3 business days)
Stage 5: Publish    → Scheduled in CMS for target date
Stage 6: Promote    → Shared on Twitter/X, newsletter, Hacker News where relevant
Stage 7: Measure    → Performance reviewed 14 days post-publish
```

Total lead time: **13 business days** from outline approval to publish.
