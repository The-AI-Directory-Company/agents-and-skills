## 2. Deal Stage Definitions

| Stage | Entry Criteria | Rep Actions | Exit Criteria | Average Time in Stage |
|-------|---------------|-------------|---------------|----------------------|
| **Prospecting** | [Matches ICP firmographic and behavioral signals] | [Research account, personalize outreach, multi-thread contacts] | [Meeting booked with decision-relevant contact] | [X days] |
| **Discovery** | [Meeting confirmed with qualified contact] | [Run discovery call, identify pain, map stakeholders] | [Pain, timeline, and budget situation understood] | [X days] |
| **Qualification** | [Pain confirmed, stakeholders identified] | [Complete MEDDIC, confirm champion, engage economic buyer] | [Champion identified, decision process mapped, MEDDIC score >= 11] | [X days] |
| **Solution Design** | [Qualified opportunity, MEDDIC >= 11] | [Deliver tailored demo, run POC/trial, technical validation] | [Technical win confirmed — prospect agrees the product solves the problem] | [X days] |
| **Proposal** | [Technical win confirmed] | [Present pricing, negotiate terms, handle procurement] | [Verbal agreement on pricing and terms] | [X days] |
| **Negotiation / Legal** | [Verbal agreement] | [Redline contract, handle legal/security review, manage procurement] | [Contract signed] | [X days] |
| **Closed Won** | [Signed contract] | [Hand off to Customer Success, confirm onboarding timeline] | [Revenue recognized, CS onboarding started] | — |
| **Closed Lost** | [Deal lost at any stage] | [Document loss reason, schedule future check-in if appropriate] | [Loss reason recorded in CRM with specific detail] | — |

**Rules:**
- Deals cannot skip stages. If a rep cannot articulate the exit criteria for the current stage, the deal is not at that stage.
- Every stage transition must be recorded in the CRM with a date and evidence.
- "Demo completed" is an activity, not an exit criterion. The exit criterion is the buyer's response to the demo.
