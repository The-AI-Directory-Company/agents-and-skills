# Product Launch Brief: Relay Mobile App v2

## 1. Launch Summary

- **Product/Feature:** Relay Mobile v2 — rebuilt native iOS and Android app for field service management
- **Launch Date:** April 22, 2026, 9:00 AM ET
- **Launch Tier:** Tier 1 (major — press, event keynote, full campaign)
- **Launch Owner:** Priya Chandran, Director of Product Marketing
- **One-Line Pitch:** "Relay v2 gives field technicians offline-first job management, real-time parts lookup, and photo documentation — all from their phone."

## 2. Positioning

**For** field service managers and technicians at mid-market companies (50-500 field workers) **who** currently juggle paper forms, disconnected apps, and delayed job updates,
**Relay v2 provides** a single mobile app that works offline, syncs automatically, and eliminates manual data entry from the field,
**unlike** ServiceTitan Mobile or Salesforce Field Service, **it** was built offline-first for environments with unreliable connectivity — not bolted on as an afterthought.

**Website version:** "The field service app that works where your network does not. Relay v2 gives your technicians offline job management, instant parts lookup, and photo documentation — syncing everything when they are back online."

**Sales conversation version:** "Your technicians lose 45 minutes a day to paperwork and app reloads in low-connectivity areas. Relay v2 eliminates that — it is built offline-first so everything works in basements, rural sites, and elevators. When they reconnect, it syncs automatically."

**Internal alignment version:** "Relay v2 is our platform bet on mobile-first field service. It replaces the v1 wrapper app with native iOS/Android, adds offline-first architecture, and introduces parts lookup and photo documentation — the two most-requested features from the last 18 months."

## 4. Key Messages

1. **Message:** "Relay v2 works offline — no signal, no problem."
   - **Proof point:** Full offline mode tested across 14 customer environments with 99.7% sync success rate in beta.
   - **Objection:** "What if there are sync conflicts?" — Relay uses last-write-wins with conflict flagging; managers get a dashboard of any conflicts to resolve.

2. **Message:** "Photo documentation replaces paper inspection forms."
   - **Proof point:** Beta customers reduced form completion time by 62% using photo capture with auto-tagged metadata.
   - **Objection:** "Our forms are compliance-required." — Photos attach to structured inspection records that map to existing compliance templates.

3. **Message:** "Real-time parts lookup means fewer return trips."
   - **Proof point:** TechServe Corp (beta customer) reduced return-trip rate from 23% to 9% in the first month.
   - **Objection:** "Our parts catalog is in our ERP." — Relay syncs with SAP, Oracle, and NetSuite parts catalogs via our integration layer.
