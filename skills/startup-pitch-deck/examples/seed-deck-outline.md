## Slide 2: Problem

Product teams at B2B SaaS companies cannot answer: "Which features keep customers?"

Today, they cobble together SQL queries, generic analytics tools, and gut instinct. The result:

- Product managers spend 8+ hours/week on ad-hoc analysis instead of shipping
- 74% of B2B SaaS PMs say they cannot connect feature usage to retention (ProductPlan Survey, 2025)
- The average B2B SaaS company loses 32% of customers annually -- most without understanding why

---

## Slide 3: Solution

FlowMetrics connects feature adoption data to retention outcomes in real time.

Unlike generic analytics tools, FlowMetrics automatically maps which features users engage with in their first 30 days and correlates that behavior with 6-month retention.

Product teams see which features to invest in and which onboarding paths lead to long-term customers -- without writing SQL.
